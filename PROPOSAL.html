This proposal outlines the development, design, and deployment of a professional hospitality brand website along with a comprehensive admin console for content management and event curation.

Executive Summary

RÌNWÁ Hospitality & Experiences requires a sophisticated digital presence that reflects the brand's culture-first ethos and refined aesthetic. This proposal covers:

  • Website Platform: Fully responsive, SEO-optimized Next.js application with hero carousel, event showcase, gallery system, and contact management
  • Admin Console: Intuitive dashboard for managing all dynamic content including events, media, partnerships, and form submissions
  • Ongoing Support: Initial deployment, testing, and 30 days of post-launch support
Scope of Work
A. Website Development
  • Hero Carousel: Multi-media hero section with video/image support, auto-play functionality, and refined transitions
  • Brand Partners Section: Animated marquee display of partnerships with categorization by region
  • Experience Section: Featured weekly events (Monday Reset Sessions, Wednesday Wine & Dine, etc.)
  • Featured Event Block: Prominent event showcase with parallax effects, details, and RSVP capabilities
  • Past Events Gallery: Masonry-style grid of curated past events with filtering by category and year
  • Media Gallery: Lightbox gallery with video and image support, refined presentation
  • Contact/Inquiry Form: Multi-step contact form with industry selection, project goals, and submission management
  • Social Presence Section: Links to social media platforms
  • Professional Footer: Navigation, links, contact information, and compliance details
  • SEO Optimization: Schema markup (JSONLd), meta tags, sitemap, and robots.txt
  • Responsive Design: Mobile-first approach supporting all devices and screen sizes
B. Admin Console Development
  • Hero Carousel Management: Add/edit/delete slides, media uploads, preview functionality
  • Events Dashboard: Manage featured events, weekday recurring events, and past events with categorization
  • Gallery Management: Upload, organize, and manage media items with metadata
  • Brand Partnerships: Add/edit/remove partners, reorder display, manage visibility
  • Locations Management: Configure city/location options with schedules and availability
  • Form Settings: Manage contact form categories, industries, and goals
  • Inquiry Submissions: Dashboard to view, filter, and manage contact form submissions
  • Analytics Overview: Basic metrics on form submissions and engagement
  • User Management: Role-based access control for admin users
  • Content Moderation: Publish/draft workflow with live preview
C. Technology Stack
  • Frontend: Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
  • Animations: Framer Motion for smooth, professional interactions
  • Admin: React-based dashboard with intuitive UI/UX
  • Database: Backend database solution (to be confirmed)
  • Hosting: Vercel or similar modern hosting platform
  • Security: SSL/TLS encryption, secure authentication
